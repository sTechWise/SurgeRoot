Distributor Onboarding Checklist

Documents and steps required to become a SurgeRoot supply partner.

Required Documents

  • Business email from company domain
  • Company name and legal entity (W9 name)
  • Valid Resale Certificate (PDF)
  • W9 Form (PDF)
  • Company website
  • State of registration
  • Primary contact name and phone

Optional but Recommended

  • References or purchase order history (PDF)
  • Target brands you distribute
  • Estimated monthly purchase volume

Process Timeline

  • Step 1: Complete the Distributor Application online.
  • Step 2: Our team reviews submitted documents (3–7 business days).
  • Step 3: If approved, receive portal access credentials.
  • Step 4: Place your first order.

Requirements

  • Files must be PDF format, max 5MB each.
  • Business email required (no Gmail/Hotmail for primary contact).
  • Agreement to MAP and channel policies.
  • EIN reference available upon request (redacted for public documents).
