Regional Home Goods Brand — Stabilized Amazon Presence and 3x Monthly Orders

Within 90 days SurgeRoot increased weekly reorder frequency and stabilized MAP pricing, resulting in a 2.5x increase in distributor reorder velocity.

Background

HomeEase (fictional sample brand) is a mid-size US home goods brand experiencing MAP violations from unknown sellers.

Objective

Help the brand regain controlled marketplace presence and establish steady, compliant retail demand.

Approach

  • Verified brand's authorized distributor list with brand rep.
  • Opened an account, purchased initial test lot of 40 units.
  • Monitored Keepa and Buy Box behavior for 30 days and adjusted pricing strategy to align with MAP.
  • Implemented consistent replenishment cadence and shared weekly sales reports with the brand.

Results

  • Initial order sold at target velocity and price within 30 days.
  • Buy Box win rate stabilized; Amazon did not enter the listing.
  • Weekly reorder frequency increased from ad hoc to consistent 2x per week.
  • Distributor increased allocation to SurgeRoot.

Working with SurgeRoot was smooth from day one. They provided clear documentation and respected our listing rules. The steady reorder cadence helped us forecast production.
