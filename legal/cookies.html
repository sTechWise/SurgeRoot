Cookie Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how MD AL AMIN LLC dba SurgeRoot uses cookies and similar technologies on surgeroot.com.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and understand how you interact with content.

Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

  • Cookie consent preference — Remembers whether you accepted or declined cookies. Stored in your browser's localStorage.

Analytics Cookies (with consent)

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — Helps us understand how visitors interact with our website. Collects anonymized data about pages visited, time on site, and traffic sources.
  • Google Tag Manager — Manages analytics and marketing tags. Does not collect personal data directly.

Your Choices

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept — We will set analytics cookies to improve our service.
  • Decline — No analytics cookies will be set. Only essential functionality cookies will be used.

You can also manage cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.

Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics may set cookies on your device. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking at Google Analytics Opt-out.

Questions about our cookie practices? Contact us at info@surgeroot.com.
