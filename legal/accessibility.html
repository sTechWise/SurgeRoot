Accessibility Statement

MD AL AMIN LLC, operating as SurgeRoot, is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

Our Commitment

We aim to conform to WCAG 2.1 Level AA guidelines. Our efforts include:

  • Sufficient color contrast ratios throughout the site
  • Full keyboard navigation support
  • Descriptive alt text on all images
  • Proper form labels and error identification
  • Semantic HTML structure with ARIA attributes where appropriate
  • Responsive design that works across devices and screen sizes
  • Focus indicators for interactive elements

Measures We Take

  • Include accessibility as part of our design and development process
  • Test with keyboard navigation and screen reader compatibility
  • Monitor and review accessibility on an ongoing basis

Known Limitations

While we strive for full accessibility, some areas may not yet be fully optimized. We are actively working to identify and resolve any barriers.
