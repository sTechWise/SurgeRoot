About SurgeRoot

Established 2025

SurgeRoot is operated by MD AL AMIN LLC. We are a US-registered retail partner that purchases branded inventory from authorized distributors and sells to customers primarily via major online marketplaces. Our mission is to represent brands responsibly while delivering exceptional marketplace execution and consistent demand.

SurgeRoot is a US-based retail partner operated by MD AL AMIN LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company. We purchase through authorized distribution channels and responsibly represent brands across major online marketplaces.

Leadership

MD AL AMIN

MD AL AMIN is the founder and principal of MD AL AMIN LLC. With a deep focus on authorized retail distribution and marketplace operations, he built SurgeRoot to bridge the gap between brand-authorized suppliers and compliant online retail execution. His approach emphasizes transparency, documentation, and long-term partnerships over short-term volume.
