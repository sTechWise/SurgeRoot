About SurgeRoot

Established 2025

SurgeRoot is a US-based retail company focused on authorized distribution sourcing and responsible marketplace execution. We purchase branded inventory through approved wholesale channels and represent brands across major online platforms with a long-term, compliance-driven approach.

What We Do

Focus on operations, not identity.

We source branded products exclusively through authorized distribution channels. Our operations emphasize compliance, inventory integrity, and structured marketplace execution designed to preserve brand value.

Authorized Sourcing

Every product is purchased through verified, brand-approved wholesale channels.

Compliance-First Operations

MAP adherence, resale documentation, and brand channel policies strictly followed.

Inventory Integrity

Full invoice trails, W9 records, and traceable documentation for every transaction.

Structured Marketplace Execution

Optimized listings, performance-driven strategies, and disciplined execution to preserve brand value.
